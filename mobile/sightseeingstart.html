Sightseeing information for South Dalmatia

This site is a guide to activities and sightseeing available to visitors in Apartment Tedo and other good holiday homes in Komarna in South Dalmatia between Split and Dubrovnik.


The information is mainly grouped according to the direction you go when leaving Komarna. If you do not want to travel more than a few minutes, there is a page with Sights close to Komarna.


The Short Excursion page will take you a little further away and so will Historic Excursions and Hercegovina Trip.

If you feel like some action - try Canoe and Rafting.


You must see Dubrovnik! On the Dubrovnik Page, you find information about places to visit between Komarna and Dubrovnik. Except for Peljesac which has its own page.

Neretva Delta's page has information about places you meet driving on both sides of the river. Mostar page has information about sights on the road to - and close to Mostar. Hercegovina page is about what you may find if you drive a short distance into Bosnia-Hercegovina (don't miss the Kravica Waterfall).

Mljet page is about how you can arrange for a visit to this lovely island and finally the Sarajevo by Train is about what you will see if you spend a day on a special train journey.

In Historic Excursions, you may find some background information about Croatian history and suggestions to places you must visit if you are interested in history.
