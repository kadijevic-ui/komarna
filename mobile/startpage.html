Croatia Holiday Self-Catering Accommodation

Croatia is a marvelous country, and our favorite stretch of land is the riviera north of Dubrovnik.

Komarna is a former fisherman's village and lies between the towering mountains and the clear Adriatic Sea with the cleanest water you can find at the Mediterranean.



In the high season, Komarna is perfect for families with children because it is a safe and peaceful place without traffic going through.


Off-season it is ideal for couples and friends because it is close to many places of interest. Dubrovnik, Mostar and even Sarajevo and Split can be reached in a day-trip.

There are many other sightseeing opportunities, the swimming is great, and you are sure to have a fabulous vacation here.
