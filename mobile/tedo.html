Apartment Tedo

Self-Catering Holiday rental - Apartment Tedo

Apartment Tedo is an ideal holiday accommodation for friends and for families who wish a comfortable and well-equipped vacation home.


Tedo-1, Tedo-2 and Tedo-3 are non-smoking houses. You may smoke in Tedo-4.


Our village is a secure and calm place for your holiday. You can enjoy the beautiful nature, swim in clean and safe water, and you are in a good central location for travels around to the many interesting places in the surrounding area.


From both the terrace and the balcony there is a wonderful sea view over the small islands to the Peljesac Peninsula.


The Houses


Apartment Tedo is four semi-detached houses of 64 m2 each. They share a garden and two rear terraces, each terrace has a big barbecue. At the front of the house, each house has a roofed terrace at the front door and a balcony in front of the master bedroom.

At the lower floor, there is a living room and an open kitchen that is well-equipped with dishwasher and a big fridge and a freezer. The upper floor has two bedrooms with air conditioning and a bathroom with a washing machine. The master bedroom has a safe and a door to a balcony with a sea view and the family bedroom has a door to the terrace and the garden. There is free wireless internet access from all houses.

Garden


Behind the house, there is a shared garden and a terrace with a barbecue. There are two more terraces. One is with mandarin-, orange- and lemon trees and our tortoises live here.

The upper terrace has sun beds and another big barbecue.

Each house has a garden table with six chairs and extra chairs at the balcony and in front of the house.

The tortoises love to be feed with green salad and tomatoes. They are not always easy to find because they mostly sit still and are well camouflaged, but if they are thirsty or hungry, and you bring them water or some of their favorite food, they can move with a surprising speed.

Living Room and Kitchen


Downstairs there is a living room with a sofa for two persons, a coffee table and a dining table with six chairs.There is a wall mounted 32" flat screen TV with satellite receiver (Astra 1) that will receive channels in English like BBC World, Sky Channel, CNN and a large number of channels in German, Italian and French. There is also reception of many radio channels.

Under the TV there is a DVD player, some movies, many novels, maps and our book with advice and directions about how you can get the best out of your stay.



A new kitchen was installed in for 2012. It is in open connection with the living room.

There is tableware for six, big fridge, freezer, hood, electric kettle, microwave oven, coffee machine and whatever else you may need is in the drawers and cupboards.


Front Entrance


The entrance door to the living room is from the front terrace, and parking. From the entrance door and the front terrace, there is a view over the bay to the Peljesac Peninsular. There are two chairs here and a small coffee table. It is a great place to sit if you want to follow the life at the village and want to be in the shadow from the sun. The shadow is provided by big trees and sun-screens. There is a gate that can be closed so that small children can be safe for not running into the street. There is not much traffic, but the occasional car may pass by.


The Bedrooms and Bathroom


The master bedroom on the upper floor has bed side tables, closet, clock radio, air conditioning safety deposite box and exit to the balcony, overlooking the sea. There are two good chairs and a coffee table on the balcony and it is a great place to have a drink when the sun sets in the west.

In the master bedroom it is possible to have a baby bed on request.

In House #4 the bedrooms are reversed, which may be an advantage for a family with 3.4 children.



The bathroom is on the first floor, and is nicely renovated. They are comfortable and have a shower cabin, hair dryer and a small washing machine that is easy to operate.

The bathroom is between the two bedrooms and with entrance from the stair's landing.



The family bedroom has a bunk bed (full-size mattresses, 200X90cm), table, closet, air conditioning and a sofa that may be converted into two full-size beds. There is a door to the garden from this bedroom.

Both the sofa and the bunk beds are full size and comfortable for adults. In the house #4 this room has a double bed and the sofa and bunk bed is in the big bedroom.
